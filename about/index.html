MICHAEL MIRANDA
Founder & Principal ConsultantFundador y Consultor Principal

Michael Miranda is the Founder & Principal Consultant of Miranmi, a consulting and analytics practice focused on helping businesses grow with clarity, insight, and strategic direction.

He works with entrepreneurs, small businesses, and organizations to strengthen operations, improve visibility, and prepare for long-term growth — including opportunities in government contracting and data-informed decision-making.

Michael brings a background in economics, analytics, and business strategy, along with leadership experience as a U.S. Veteran. His approach combines structured thinking, practical execution, and a commitment to delivering meaningful results for the businesses and communities he serves.

He holds an MBA from the University of the Incarnate Word and a Master's degree in Economics from the University of Texas at San Antonio, with a concentration in business data analysis and forecasting.
